ABOUT

Devour the Tower was a created for a senior design competition while I was in college. It won first place in the computer science division of the competition. I worked with 6 other students to develop the game in Unity. The original version had rewarded ads (extra life), leaderboards, and was available on the iOS App Store and the Google Play Store. It has since been taken down due to lack of support, but I wanted to showcase it so I stripped the mobile-only features and reuploaded it to Itch.io.
